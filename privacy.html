Privacy Policy

TaxDays LLC ("we", "us") operates the TaxDays mobile application. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Data We Collect

TaxDays does not collect or transmit any personal data to our servers. All trip records, dates, and settings you enter are stored locally on your device and, if enabled, synced privately via Apple iCloud (CloudKit) — accessible only to you.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account under Apple's privacy and security policies. TaxDays LLC has no access to this data.

Subscription & Payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not track your usage behavior.

Children's Privacy

TaxDays is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
